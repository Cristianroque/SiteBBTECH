First

FIRST® is a robotics community that engages students in team- and mentor-based STEAM programs and exciting sports competitions.

Through a suite of programs, students develop comprehensive life skills including creative problem solving, leadership, communication, and most importantly, self-confidence and a sense of belonging.

FIRST Robotics Competition

The FIRST Robotics Competition is an international competition of the non-profit organization FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) that combines the adrenaline of sports with the rigor of science and technology.

For the development of the robot, the team works with a basic kit and is free to use its creativity, as long as it respects the rules of 59.05 inches tall, 119.05 pounds, maximum amount of motors, actuators, and some materials.
