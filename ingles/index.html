What is BBTECH #9046?

The SESI SENAI BBTECH, known as FRC9046, is a team from Maceió, city of Alagoas. It was the first, and until today, is the only team from Alagoas to compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition, renowned worldwide as FRC. Currently, the team comprises eight competitors, three instructors and five mentors, who pass through several stages of a selection process and, today, are responsible for all demands concerning the competition: building the robot, producing the documentation, promoting events, and connecting with companies and sponsors.


Using the name RED & BLUE, the current BBTECH, emerged in the second half of 2021, just 2 months before the Off-Season Brazil 2021, the Infinite Recharge. Even with a short training period, the team managed to structure itself in such a way as to win second place in the Rookie All-Star award. Later, in the Off-Season 2022, called Rapid React, the team again won an important award, the Gracious Professionalism, for those who, even inserted in a competitive context, can maintain respect and care for others, showing ethics and discipline in the professional context.

Mission

Stand out among other teams, delivering robots, documents and events that go down in history.
