Sister Teams-FIRST® Tech Challenge

SESI SENAI FTBEN

FTBEN #21077 is a robotics team from the state of Alagoas that competes in the FTC (First Tech Challenge) tournaments, competition held by FIRST®.Our team emerged in late 2021, in the city of Maceió-AL with the goal of expanding FIRST competitions in our state and provide young people with a more immersive experience in engineering and community work.

We participated in the Freight Frenzy season and won the 2nd place prize for Creative Thinking, which is awarded to the team that best reflects the journey they took during the process of building the engineering project.

SESI FTCAMB

SESI FTCamb #16049 is a team that belongs to the city of Maceió - AL. It became the best team in the country by winning the main award of the First Tech Challenge championship promoted by FIRST, the Inspire Award, in the year 2022 during the FREIGHT FRENZY season. It is worth noting that the award is given to the team that has become an inspiration within the competition, that has competed for all the official awards and excelled in each of them.

The team emerged in 2018 and currently has 7 members in its formation. Finally, this competition promotes surreal experiences that have a positive impact on the lives of our competitors, such as developing skills in communication, conflict resolution, decision making, and logical reasoning.

Partner Teams-FIRST® Robotics Competition

SESI SENAI POTIBOT

P0T1BOT is the first FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) team from Rio Grande Do Norte, was created in the year 2021 and has this name as a way to represent the region, specifically the Poti Guaçu native tribe.

P0T1BOT is the first FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) team from Rio Grande Do Norte, was created in the year 2021 and has this name as a way to represent the region, specifically the Poti Guaçu native tribe. The team participated in the OFF-SEASON Brazil 2021/2022 and brought home the prizes: Dean's list, Gracious Professionalism and the TEAM SPIRIT-AWARD. The team, which has 10 members, 2 coaches and 12 mentors to foster in its history, has as its main mission the dissemination of FIRST values, and the appreciation of STEAM, where we stand out in our main projects: the POT1CAST, meetings broadcast on YouTube of the team itself in which we can make connections with other teams and representatives of STEAM, and the P0T1TRIP, where we take the knowledge of first in other schools such which has no or low knowledge about robotics.

SESI SENAI MEGAZORD

SESI SENAI MEGAZORD #7563 is a robotics team from the state of São Paulo, formed by 10 members, which participates in FRC (First Robotics Competition) tournaments organized by the institution FIRST.

Our team emerged in 2018, in the city of Jundiaí-SP with the goal of making a difference in our community, spreading FIRST values and STEM knowledge. In our last competition, MEGAZORD won the Engineering Inspiration-Award, which indicates that we are doing a great job in integrating science and technology in our society.

SESI SENAI OCTOPUS

A The team SESI/SENAI Octopus #7567 was created in August 2018. Our mission is to impact our community through FIRST and STEAM by influencing and improving the lives of young people to become great leaders in science and technology. We are inspired by FIRST's values and by the opportunity to transform lives and generations.

Our team members and mentors work in two major areas: Marketing (social media, scouting, design, social projects, and documentation) and Automation (electrical, mechanical, and programming), forming the 8 tentacles of our octopus.

Under Control

The Under Control 1156 team, Novo Hamburgo, Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. Team founded in 2002 at the school Marista Pio XII, the team participates in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). Under Control has 20 years of experience, in 2021 was a world finalist of the Chairmans Award, in 2018 2x regional winner and 7x Chairmans winner in regional.

hin Under Control, there are several technical and non-technical areas. Among the technical ones, we have: Robot Design, Mechanics, Pneumatics, Electrical, Programming and Scouting/Data Analysis. The non-technical areas, on the other hand, are made up of: Logistics, Marketing, Award Writing, Media, Fundraising, and Advocacy. So there is room for everyone within a single robotics team

SESI SENAI TUCANUS
